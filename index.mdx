## Why Zenda?

Coordinating appointments without a dedicated tool creates unnecessary friction:
- Reservations that never get confirmed, leaving gaps in your schedule
- Patients who forget their sessions
- Your valuable time lost in back-and-forth messaging
- No organized record of patient history

Zenda solves all of this in one centralized platform.
